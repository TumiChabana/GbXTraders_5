Markets and assets to trade

Cryptocurrency

Trade shares to speculate on company stocks without taking ownership. You'll take a position on the direction of the share price by going short or long.

Indices

Get exposure to an entire economy trading indices with us. You'll speculate on the performance of groups of shares by going short or long on rising or falling markets.

ETFs

Speculate on exchange traded funds or ETFs, which are investment baskets that track the value of individual commodities as well as a collection of stocks.

Forex

Take a long or short position on the conversion of one currency into another. There are several types of forex pairs you can trade against each other.

Thematic and Basket

Form a unique portfolio by grouping certain stocks to speculate via thematic and basket trading. Your portfolio will be based on certain trends in which you take a position on stocks that form part of a theme.

Commodities

Choose from a variety of physical goods to buy or sell on the exchange. Trade commodities from different industries, from agriculture and energy to minerals.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start trading?
To start trading, you need to open a trading account with a broker, deposit funds, and begin trading. Research and practice are essential.
How can beginners learn to trade?
Beginners can learn to trade by taking online courses, reading trading books, participating in webinars, and using demo accounts to practice without risking real money.
What are the best trading platforms?
The best trading platforms vary based on individual needs but popular ones include MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, and various proprietary platforms offered by brokers.
How can I start trading stocks and shares?
To start trading stocks and shares, you'll need to open an account with a stockbroker, research stocks to invest in, and use the broker's platform to buy and sell shares.
How can I start trading forex and currencies?
To start trading forex and currencies, you need to open a forex trading account with a broker, deposit funds, and trade currency pairs. It's important to learn about forex markets and strategies.
